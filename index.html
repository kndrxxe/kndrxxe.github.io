Projects

Liliw RHU Vaccination Records System

This system is a web-based application that aims to automate the process of managing the vaccination records of the residents of Liliw, Laguna. It is designed to help the Rural Health Unit (RHU) staff to easily manage and access the vaccination records of the residents.

Inventory System

This project is a comprehensive sales inventory system designed to streamline the management of sales and inventory for businesses. It includes features such as product tracking, sales reporting, and inventory control to ensure efficient and accurate business operations.

World Traveler Magazine

This project is a digital magazine that showcases travel destinations around the world. It features articles, photos, and travel tips to inspire and inform travelers.

OYC Inventory System

This system is a web-based application that aims to automate the process of managing the inventory of a business. It is designed to help business owners and staff to easily manage and access the information of their products.

(E-BIPMS) Electronic Barangay Integrated Profiling and Monitoring System

This system is a web-based application that aims to automate the process of profiling and monitoring the residents of a barangay. It is designed to help barangay officials and staff to easily manage and access the information of their constituents.
